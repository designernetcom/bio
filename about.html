Welcome to Bioclix Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals

Bioclix Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals is the innovation-driven animal nutrition and health care company in Gujrat (India). We develop innovative solutions for optimum productivity and well being of Poultry animals. We are a team of poultry medicines experts, nutritionists, technical consultants, researchers and functional staff. A balanced mix of experienced and young minds gives us the business edge for today, at the same time keeping us geared up for tomorrow’s challenges.

Since2011
Smart Solutions for Poultry

The quality of animal feed is typically judged based on its nutritional value and overall appearance. Maintaining quality and nutritional value in feed cannot be achieved solely through sound harvest and storage practices, however. Producers must also consider solutions specially designed to protect feed value.

Adding Bioclix Chemicals Feed Quality solutions to your operation can help prolong feed shelf-life, reduce production costs and improve animal performance.

Bioclix Chemicals strongly believes that to remain effective in an increasingly competitive market, it must stay at the forefront of developments and offer products and services, which consumers trust and believe to be value for money.

Despite its aim to change and adapt for the future, Bioclix Chemicals will never change the belief that employees are its most valuable assets. Bioclix Chemicals is proud to have to its credit several awards from different institutions on management, finance and other areas consecutively for past several years.

Bioclix Chemicals grew up with a strong heritage as a research based company. As Bioclix Chemicals move into the 21st century, the future is becoming increasingly research driven to meet the needs of healthcare of livestock industry.
